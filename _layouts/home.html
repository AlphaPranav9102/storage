Team 4099

The Falcons

Team 4099 is a FIRST robotics team based out of Poolesville High School. We provide an inclusive, student-led environment for students to learn real-world engineering skills.

Introducting Kestrel

Intake

Flexible, pneumatics-based intake with four bar actuation and an automated LED system for count of cargo.

Climber

Two telescoping arms and two pivoting arms climber for traversal rung. Achieved a consistent automated climb under 12 seconds.

Shooter

Fixed-position shooter hood designed for fender shots. Reaches 45 feet high at full power.

Drivetrain

MK4i flipped swerve modules with a L2 gear ratio, drivetrain with low center of gravity for good defense.
